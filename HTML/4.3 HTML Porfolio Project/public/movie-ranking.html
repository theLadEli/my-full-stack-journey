Eli's Favourite Movies

My top 3 movies of all time.

JoJo Rabbit

I've watched it like five times, it's both hilarious and informative on the war. Before watching it I was pretty critical of a WWII comedy film, but after watching it I couldn't recommend it more!

All Quiet on the Western Front

This was both chilling and insightful at the same time. It showed the war from german soldiers perspective, illustrating the false glorification soldiers were impressioned.

Lord of the Rings

Although it's not a movie but a trilogy, it still deserves a position on this list.
